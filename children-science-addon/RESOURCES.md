# Resources

## Knowledge

- **Khan Academy: Ecosystems and Ecology** (Khan Academy) — Animated, structured video lessons on food chains, food webs, energy flow, and ecosystem types. Excellent for 10-14 year olds. Start here for foundational content.
- **National Geographic Kids: Ecosystems** (National Geographic) — Visual, kid-friendly explanations with photographs of real ecosystems. Great for concrete examples and inspiration.
- **PBS Learning Media: Ecosystem Activities** (PBS) — Vetted, free activities and interactive modules designed for upper elementary/middle school. High production quality.
- **Crash Course Kids: Food Chains & Ecosystems** (YouTube) — Energetic, accessible crash course videos (5-10 min). Good for hooks and engagement.
- **NOAA: Ocean Ecosystems Basics** (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) — Primary source for ocean ecosystem specifics. Authoritative and freely available.

## Wisdom (Communities)

- **r/AskScience** (Reddit) — Good for vetting unusual questions learners might ask. Moderated by domain experts.
- **iNaturalist Community** (iNaturalist.org) — Real scientists identifying species and discussing local ecosystems. Excellent for connecting learning to real observation.
